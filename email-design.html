Crafting Compelling Email Designs: Elevating Brands and Discounts for Click-Worthy Campaigns



Streamlined Email Design

In my role as a senior graphic designer, I routinely received email design requests that centered around showcasing brands and promotions, often accompanied by enticing discounts. These requests presented a dual challenge: incorporating both lifestyle and product images effectively to maximize user engagement and drive sales.


Streamlined Email Design

Throughout the design process, I adhered to fundamental design principles, ensuring that key elements such as the brand logo, product clusters, discount information, and compelling calls-to-action (CTAs) occupied prime real estate within the email's first fold. This strategic placement aimed to encourage users to click, explore, and take advantage of the offered discounts.


High-Converting and Visually Stunning Email Designs

Additionally, I recognized the importance of product image enhancement, employing Photoshop techniques to retouch and optimize visuals. I also paid meticulous attention to selecting backgrounds that seamlessly blended with the email's hero section, enhancing overall visual appeal.

While most of my email designs were crafted within Marketo, I also leveraged other content management systems (CMS) like HubSpot to create templates and various marketing emails. This holistic approach ensured that every email campaign not only looked visually appealing but also effectively conveyed the brand's message and promotional offers.
