"Free Shipping" Marketing Campaign


The objective of this campaign was to boost sales and attract more memberships for the digital ADI store. As a Senior Graphic Designer, I conceptualized a range of collateral pieces, including email designs, web banners, marketing tiles, digital signage for in-store monitors, and mobile app banners.



Approach:

Drawing inspiration from the idea of a shipping truck, I incorporated visual elements such as a box and dynamic lines to convey a sense of speed and efficiency. These creative choices aimed to capture the attention of the target audience and emphasize the convenience and fast delivery associated with the free shipping offer.


Result:

For the campaign, I employed a contrast of cool and warm colors as the background and incorporated a vibrant yellow shade in the logotype to create a visually striking effect. You can explore the final results of these design choices in the deliverables showcased below.
