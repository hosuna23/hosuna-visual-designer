Project: E.L.I.S. Website Design



E.L.I.S. is a privately owned warehousing company based in El Paso, Texas, offering comprehensive commercial storage solutions. As a senior graphic designer, I took on the task of highlighting the significance of our operations and services to meet various business requirements. The owner, though aware of the importance, needed assistance in effectively conveying the corporate storyline and related content. To address this, I developed a client persona to precisely identify our target audience. Subsequently, I crafted a compelling business narrative that aligns with the user experience, resulting in a captivating and informative website.

1. Issues:

The small-business owner operating in El Paso, a highly competitive border town for warehousing services, required an effective means to promote her business. To successfully attract clients, the website needed to incorporate three essential elements. Firstly, it should prominently display contact information, emphasizing convenience and the advantageous location. Secondly, it should highlight key differentiators, such as having certified forklift operators, to set the business apart from competitors. Lastly, it should present an appealing and comprehensive list of warehousing services offered.

2. Approach:

Persona
The target audience for this website comprises males between the ages of 30 and 45. A significant portion of this demographic consists of small-business owners who require both short- and long-term storage solutions for their merchandise. It is crucial to emphasize that our warehouse offers 24/7 availability to accommodate the needs of businesses and ensure the safety of their stored goods.

3. Wireframe Specifics:

To enhance user experience, the website sections were structured to provide clear identification of services and pricing. The menu was designed for easy navigation, ensuring that relevant information is readily accessible. Strategically placed calls-to-action were incorporated throughout the site, encouraging users to contact the warehouse company instantly, thereby maximizing potential business opportunities.


4. Wireframe Overview:

Website highlights include:

  • Type hierarchy Roboto -helps emphasize the different sections.
  • Calls-to- action are colored-coded to entice the user to click.
  • Site navigation ensures information is readily accessible.
