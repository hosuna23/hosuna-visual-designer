Seasonal Marketing Campaign Strategy: Boosting Sales for Memorial Day and Canada Day




Problem:

Every year, ADI runs seasonal campaigns to celebrate holidays and implements various discounts and offers to boost sales. I was assigned the task of spearheading the "Memorial Day Holiday" and "Canada Day" campaigns, targeting the Canadian audience. The challenge was to devise a concept that would effectively convey nationalistic and patriotic sentiments to honor our military personnel for their service.


Approach:

To address this challenge, I began by creating a concept board encompassing various ideas. I collaborated closely with the creative director and the global user experience leader, presenting multiple concepts for evaluation and alignment. To streamline the brainstorming process, I utilized Figma to develop a comprehensive mood board that helped refine the most suitable approach.

Result:

Upon receiving approval for the chosen approach, I incorporated patriotic elements, such as stars for the American version and the Maple leaf for the Canadian version. I carefully selected blue for the U.S. campaign and a dark burgundy-red for the Canadian campaign, serving as distinct identifiers for each. Using a combination of Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma, I designed and optimized each image for both digital and print use.

Once the marketing team granted final approval, I successfully delivered a total of 13 assets for the U.S. and 13 for Canada, totaling 26 deliverables, each scheduled and provided to their respective websites and departments.
