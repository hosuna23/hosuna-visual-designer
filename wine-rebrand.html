Strategic Packaging Solutions: Diverse Deliverables for Wine Companies



Project: Redesign of a Local Winery

As a Senior Graphic Designer, I took a strategic approach to enhance customer experience by utilizing watercolor marks to differentiate flavors effectively.

By incorporating three distinct watercolor marks, I made it effortless for customers to identify various flavors. Each mark was thoughtfully designed to represent the unique characteristics of the respective flavor, allowing for easy recognition and enhancing the overall visual appeal.

To achieve a clean, modern, and elegant look, I employed a hand-written lettering style for the logotype. This choice was made to convey a sense of sophistication and to establish a visual identity that aligns with the brand's desired image. The handcrafted nature of the lettering adds a personal touch, while still maintaining a professional and refined aesthetic.


In order to create a tactile experience, I carefully selected a special type of paper with a textured surface for the labels. This deliberate choice aimed to give the labels a rough feel, simulating the texture of watercolor art. By doing so, the wine labels were transformed into unique pieces of artwork, further enhancing their visual appeal and setting them apart from competitors.

Throughout this project, my goal was to create wine labels that not only accurately represented the flavors but also evoked a sense of artistic beauty. By combining watercolor marks, hand-written lettering, and textured paper, I successfully achieved a harmonious blend of aesthetics and functionality.

As a Senior Graphic Designer, I continuously seek innovative ways to elevate brands and create memorable experiences for customers.
