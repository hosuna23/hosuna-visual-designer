New identity:

Months of diligent research and brainstorming went into our brand transformation journey. We meticulously crafted user personas to align personalities with our company's mission. Among the numerous ideas we explored, it was our burning desire to shape the future of the industry that truly breathed life into our brand.

Communication lies at the heart of our business, and our purpose is to propel it into the future. Our previous brand symbolized thinking "outside the box," but we made a strategic shift. Now, our focus is on the remarkable impact we create for our clients and their customers by propelling communications forward.

Since the revitalization of our brand, we have experienced a remarkable 500% increase in qualified leads. Prospective agents are now engaging with us at significantly higher rates. Our pioneering brand champion program has fostered a sense of pride and engagement among our employees. We are currently undergoing a massive overhaul of all internal applications to enhance the user experience both internally and externally, optimizing our operations.
