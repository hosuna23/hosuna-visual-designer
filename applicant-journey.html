Applicant Journey Map


As a senior graphic designer, I had the opportunity to work on an exciting project focused on the applicant journey for Working Solutions. With a keen understanding of the importance of user experience, I undertook the task of enhancing the visual appeal and usability of the company's application process.



Through careful collaboration with the director in marketing, HR team and project stakeholders, I analyzed the existing applicant journey to identify pain points and areas for improvement. Utilizing my expertise in user-centric design, I crafted a captivating and intuitive visual design that seamlessly guides applicants through each stage of the process.

Implementing eye-catching visuals and strategic use of colors, typography, and imagery, I created an immersive experience that captures the essence of Working Solutions' brand. The key elements of the applicant journey, such as application forms, onboarding materials, and training resources, were thoughtfully designed to ensure easy navigation and accessibility.

To further streamline the process, I integrated interactive features, including progress trackers and personalized dashboards, allowing applicants to monitor their journey and stay engaged throughout. Clear calls-to-action and intuitive user interfaces were strategically placed to guide applicants to important resources, such as emails, different used platforms, stages and important time-frames.

The final result of this applicant journey project is a visually stunning and user-friendly experience that reflects the professionalism and commitment of Working Solutions. By optimizing the application process, the company is now able to attract and retain top talent, ensuring a seamless transition from applicant to valued team member.

If you are looking for a senior graphic designer who can transform your applicant journey, maximize user engagement, and create visually striking designs that align with your brand, I am confident in my ability to deliver exceptional results.
